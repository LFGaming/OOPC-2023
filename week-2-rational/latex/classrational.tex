\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
rational ADT 

This is an ADT that implements rational (fractional) values. The counter and denominator are stored as two (signed) integers. The appropriate constructors and operators are provided.

Rational values are always simplied (reduced)\+: the counter and denominator have no common factors.
